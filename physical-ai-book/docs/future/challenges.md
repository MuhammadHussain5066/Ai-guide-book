# Challenges and Limitations

Despite rapid progress, AI and robotics face significant challenges that must be addressed for widespread adoption.

## 1. Technical Challenges

### Hardware Limitations

#### Battery Life
- **Problem**: Limited operation time (2-8 hours typically)
- **Impact**: Restricts autonomous operation
- **Solutions in development**:
  - Better battery technology (solid-state)
  - Energy-efficient components
  - Wireless charging systems
  - Solar integration

#### Actuator Performance
- **Problem**: Motors lack human muscle efficiency
- **Current limitations**:
  - Heavy weight for required power
  - Heat generation
  - Limited dexterity
- **Research directions**:
  - Artificial muscles
  - Soft robotics
  - Lightweight materials
