# History of Humanoid AI

The journey of humanoid robotics spans decades of innovation, from early mechanical automata to today's sophisticated AI-powered robots.

## Timeline

### 1950s-1960s: Early Concepts
- Basic mechanical humanoid designs
- Limited mobility and functionality

### 1970s-1980s: First Walking Robots
- **WABOT-1** (1973): First full-scale humanoid robot
- Development of bipedal locomotion

### 1990s-2000s: Advanced Mobility
- **ASIMO** (2000): Honda's breakthrough in balance and walking
- **QRIO** by Sony: Entertainment-focused humanoid

### 2010s-Present: AI Integration
- **Sophia** (2016): First robot with citizenship
- **Atlas**: Advanced parkour and athletic capabilities
- Integration of machine learning and computer vision

## Key Milestones

1. **1973**: WABOT-1 - First anthropomorphic robot
2. **2000**: ASIMO - Natural human-like walking
3. **2013**: Atlas - Dynamic balance in rough terrain
4. **2016**: Sophia - Social AI integration
5. **2020+**: Commercial humanoid robots entering market
